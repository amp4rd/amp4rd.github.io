ELIXIR Service Bundle: AMP4RD

ELIXIR Service Bundles (SB) are a concept to thematically link tools, services, traning materials, and people, based on specific use cases. The ELIXIR Service Bundle "Assessing Molecular Pathogenicity for Rare Diseases" (AMP4RD) aims to define the procedures and guidelines for the annotation and interpretation of the genetic variants in the human genome. Our initiative aims to implement an integrated infrastructure for characterizing the relationship between genotype and phenotype both from the clinical and research perspectives.

The AMP4RD SB focuses on specific scientific domains related to the study of genes, proteins and their interactions. The SB identifies the key services for delivering computing workflows and tools for the annotation and interpretation of the genetic variants. In detail our initiative aims to integrate 3 main use cases:

  • Identify supporting evidences to define genotype-phenotype relationships.
  • Predict the impact of genetic variations on protein structure and function.
  • Characterize the molecular mechanisms underlying rare diseases.

According to the use cases described above we defined two specific SBs for:

  • Generating a disease report starting from DNAseq data.
  • Predicting the impact of genetic variants at the protein level.

For the practical implementation of the SBs we collected a set of resources and tools already included in the ELIXIR bio.tools registry for addressing each task.
