AMP4RD Service Bundle

Service Bundle (SB) is a new mechanism to identify how ELIXIR platforms and communities are able to address specific user needs that are not directly satisfied by the current infrastructures offered by ELIXIR. We have implemented a proposal for a Rare Disease Service Bundle (AMP4RD) to define procedures and guidelines for the annotation and interpretation of the genetic variants in the human genome. Our initiative aims to implement an integrated infrastructure for characterizing the relationship between genotype and phenotype both from the clinical and research perspectives.

Within ELIXIR the AMP4RD Service Bundle focuses on specific scientific domains related to the study of genes, proteins and their interactions. The SB identifies the ELIXIR core data resources for delivering computing workflows and tools for the annotation and interpretation of the genetic variants. In detail our initiative aims to integrate 3 main use cases:

  • Identify supporting evidences to define genotype-phenotype relationships.
  • Predict the impact of genetic variations on protein structure and function.
  • Characterize the molecular mechanisms underlying genetic diseases.

According to the use cases described above we defined two workflows for:

  • Generating a disease report starting from DNAseq data.
  • Predict di impact of genetic variants at protein level.

For the practical implementation of the workflows we collected a set of resources and tools already included in the ELIXIR bio.tools registry for addressing each task.
